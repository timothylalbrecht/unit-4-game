Crystal Collector Game

Game Instructions


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game, the Goal Number.
  2. There are four crystals to the right:
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the goal number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the goal number.
  6. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
